# Pricing And Promotions

## Base Pricing

- Quotes always include base rent, deposit, application fee, administration fee, and optional recurring add-ons.
- Monthly utilities are estimated separately and are not part of the official quote.

## Promotions

- `SPRING6` waives the administration fee for leases of 12 months or longer.
- `LOOKLEASE` gives $250 off the first full month for tours booked within 72 hours of quoting.
- Promotions cannot be stacked unless the pricing system explicitly marks them as combinable.

## Quote Validity

- Quotes are valid for 72 hours unless inventory changes sooner.
- The leasing team should clearly call out both monthly recurring charges and one-time move-in costs.
