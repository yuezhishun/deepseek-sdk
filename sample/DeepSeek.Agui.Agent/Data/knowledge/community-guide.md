# Community Guide

## Harbor Pointe

- Located near the waterfront business district.
- Best fit for renters seeking studio or one-bedroom homes with short commutes.
- Amenities include coworking lounge, package lockers, rooftop grill area, and attached parking.

## Maple Terrace

- Family-oriented community with larger floor plans and quiet courtyards.
- Best fit for renters prioritizing two-bedroom layouts, pet runs, and school access.
- Amenities include dog wash station, playground, fitness room, and storage lockers.

## Skyline Lofts

- Urban mid-rise with premium finishes and flexible lease terms.
- Best fit for renters who care about modern kitchens, concierge support, and furnished options.
- Amenities include club room, bike storage, smart locks, and EV charging.
