# Leasing FAQ

## Application Process

- Applicants must provide government ID, proof of income, and the last two pay stubs.
- Standard screening takes 1-2 business days after all documents are submitted.
- Guarantors are allowed when household income is below 2.5x monthly rent.

## Move-In Costs

- Standard deposit is one month's base rent.
- Application fee is $45 per adult.
- Administration fee is a one-time $150 charge due at lease signing.

## Pets and Parking

- Up to two pets are allowed in pet-friendly buildings.
- Pet rent is $35/month per pet and a one-time pet fee of $300 applies.
- Reserved garage parking is $125/month when available.

## Tour Policy

- Self-guided tours require a mobile number and photo ID.
- Agent-led tours are available Monday through Saturday from 9 AM to 6 PM local time.
- Same-day tours may be offered if at least 2 hours remain before the requested time slot.
